# Dashboard & Overview

The **Overview** (`/`) is the landing page — a live snapshot of your server. It's available to every
logged-in user regardless of permissions.

> 📸 **Screenshot:** _The Overview home page with stat cards, sparklines, and the online-players list._
> _(See [how to add screenshots](Images).)_

## What's on the Overview

- **Stat cards with sparklines** — online players, TPS, memory usage, and total known players, each
  with a mini trend chart drawn from the same sampled data as [[Analytics|Analytics-and-Heatmaps]].
- **Online players list** — click any player to jump straight to their [[profile|Players]].
- **Server info** — Bukkit/Paper version, EssentialsX version, uptime, MOTD, and currency symbol.
- **Recent activity** — the latest [[audit log|Audit-Log]] entries.
- **Quick actions** — one-click shortcuts to Players, Console, Economy and Broadcast.
- **Live updates** — player join/leave events arrive in real time over the SSE stream; the rest of the
  overview refreshes on a short poll.

## Command palette & navigation

Press **⌘K** (macOS) or **Ctrl K** (Windows/Linux) anywhere in the panel to open the **command
palette** — a fast fuzzy search to jump to any page or player without reaching for the mouse. The
left **sidebar** groups every feature; a user's sidebar only shows pages their
[[permissions|Permissions-Reference]] allow.

## Themes & branding

Toggle **dark / light** mode from the top bar (your choice is remembered per browser). Admins can
change the panel name, accent color and logo on the [[Branding|Branding]] page.

## Analytics

For historical **trends** (players, TPS, memory, economy, chunks, entities), a **login activity
heatmap**, and a **player geography** breakdown, see the dedicated
**[[Analytics & Heatmaps|Analytics-and-Heatmaps]]** page.
